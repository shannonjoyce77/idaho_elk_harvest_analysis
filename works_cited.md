# Works Cited
All data used in this project was sourced from the Idaho Department of Fish and Game (IDFG). This project would not have been possible without their commitment to transparent, publicly accessible wildlife management data.

---

### Data Sources
**Idaho Elk Harvest Records (2001-2024)** Idaho Department of Fish and Game - Hunt Planner Statistics

Annual elk harvest data including total harvest, hunter numbers, success rates, days hunted, antlered and antlerless harvest, spike rates, and six point or greater rates broken down by region unit and take method.

- Data accessed by year via the IDFG Hunt Planner:
[https://idfg.idaho.gov/ifwis/huntplanner/stats/?season=general&game=elk&yr=2024](https://idfg.idaho.gov/ifwis/huntplanner/stats/?season=general&game=elk&yr=2024)

---

**Idaho Hunting Unit Shapefile** Idaho Department of Fish and Game - Map Center

Spatial data for Idaho hunting unit boundaries used to create the regional harvest and trophy bull maps in Tableau.

- Shapefile accessed via the IDFG Map Center:
[https://idfg.idaho.gov/ifwis/huntplanner/mapcenter/](https://idfg.idaho.gov/ifwis/huntplanner/mapcenter/)
